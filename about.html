The Designer

Meet Angel

Founder & Principal Interior Designer

An interior designer since 2007 in Montreal, Canada, my inspiration comes from the changing of the seasons. I love the challenge of decorating and furnishing homes to suit the taste of my clients, often with a “French” air.

I moved to Florida in 2012 to continue creating spectacular environments for all budgets. I believe that luxury is not just about expensive things — it is about comfort, intention, and creating an environment that feels uniquely yours.
