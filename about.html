About Us

Crafting spaces that tell your unique story.

Meet Angel

Founder & Principal Designer

An interior designer since 2007 in Montreal, Canada, my inspiration comes from the changing of the seasons. I love the challenge of decorating and furnishing homes to suit the taste of my clients, often with a "French" air.

I moved to Florida in 2012 to continue to create spectacular environments for all budgets. What started as a passion for rearranging my childhood bedroom has evolved into a full-service design firm dedicated to elevating the way people experience their homes.

I believe that luxury is not just about expensive things; it is about comfort, intention, and creating an environment that feels uniquely yours.

Our Philosophy

Our Mission

Creating timeless interiors that inspire comfort, beauty, and connection. We strive to elevate the everyday by bringing intention and artistry into your home.

Our Values

Integrity, personalization, and timeless elegance. We prioritize understanding your lifestyle to deliver designs that are both beautiful and deeply functional.

The Approach

A collaborative, transparent journey from concept to installation. We handle the complexities so you can enjoy the seamless realization of your dream space.
